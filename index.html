About Us

Welcome to Super Subs, the South African-owned sub sandwich haven that is revolutionizing the way you enjoy your favorite handheld delight! Established in 2023, our passionate team set out on a mission to bring the ultimate sub experience to the vibrant culinary landscape of South Africa.

At Super Subs, we believe that a sub sandwich is more than just a meal — it's an art form! We take pride in our commitment to excellence, from the moment we source the freshest ingredients to the second you sink your teeth into one of our mouthwatering creations.

As a South African-owned company, we are deeply rooted in our local community. We proudly support local farmers and suppliers.

Join us at Super Subs and embark on a sub adventure like no other. We are honored to serve you and share in the joy of exceptional flavors, South African pride, and the artistry of the sub sandwich. Indulge in a sub experience that will leave you craving more, one bite at a time.
